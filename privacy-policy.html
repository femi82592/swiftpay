Process

Four steps.
That's it.

01
Create your profile
Sign up in 60 seconds. No bank account required to get started — just your name and phone number.
02
Share your QR or link
Get a personal QR code and payment link. Print it, display it, or tap-to-pay with NFC at your station.
03
Customers tip instantly
No app download needed on their end. They scan, choose an amount, and pay with Apple Pay, card, or Venmo.
04
Cash out in seconds
Transfer to any debit card, bank, or digital wallet — including international accounts — in under 3 seconds.
